CYBERBLITZ

Rajagiri school of engineering and Technology
in collaboration with A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Technological University

Cyberblitz is the association of the Computer Science & Engineering department.

Cyberblitz plays its role of giving opportunities and exposure for the young minds

of the department to the tech world through different events like Technical Talks,

Group Discussions, TechXtreme, Technical Quizzes, Cyberblitz Magazine,

Workshops etc. Cyberblitz is run by the student coordinators under

the guidance of faculty members from the department.
